STEPHEN CURRY STATS

Stephen "Steph" Curry is American basketball player that plays point guard for the Golden State Warriors. He was drafted to the NBA in 2009 and has played in
11 seasons in which he has earned three championships, two Most Valuable Player trophies, and six All-Star nominations. This website will visualize
Steph Curry's player stats across three of his seasons and show how he has improved and evolved as a player.
